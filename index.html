My Love,

Hi love, One month na since umamin ako sa’yo, and I still can’t stop smiling whenever I think about it. Grabe, ang bilis ng panahon, pero ang saya ng feeling na may tayo na ganito. Parang ang simple lang nung simula, pero ngayon, you’ve already become one of my favorite parts of every day.

You make my days brighter just by being you. Kahit simpleng “kumain ka na?” or “ingat ka” mo, napapangiti na ako. Minsan nga, nai-imagine ko lang yung smile mo, and I can’t help but grin like an idiot. Kinikilig talaga ako sa’yo, and I think about you way more than I should admit.

I love the way you laugh, the way you tease me, and even the way you’re quiet sometimes but still make me feel special. You’re addicting in the best way, and I honestly don’t want this feeling to ever stop. You make everything feel lighter, happier, and more exciting.

One month pa lang, but I already feel something real and special. I can’t wait to see where this goes, kasi I really want more moments like this… more laughs, more late-night chats, more kilig, more of you.

— Your love, Mico
